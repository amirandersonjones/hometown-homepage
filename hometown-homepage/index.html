Visit Park Forest

Home to one of the most popular outdoor swimming complexes in the state!

The 2,000 acres of parks, recreation facilities, and open land put Park Forest amongst the largest per capita in Illinois. It has umerous playgrounds, ball fields, and picnic facilities, the Village contains an abundance of tennis courts

Top three activities to do in Park Forest

Visit the aqua center
Since 1952, the Park Forest Aqua Center has been one of Chicagoland's premiere outdoor pool experiences.
Pay a trip to the wetlands
Park Forest began the process of restoring a 45-acre wetland site at its Central Park in early 2000.
Attend your local farmer's market
The Market hosts 36 full spaces with a variety of produce, bakery goods, and herbs.
